# Superstore Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a global superstore dataset containing more than **51,000 sales records**. The objective is to explore sales trends, identify high-performing products, and understand the relationship between discount strategies and profitability.

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## Dataset

The dataset contains:

* 51,290 rows
* 27 columns
* Information about products, sales, profit, region, and discounts.

## Key Analysis Performed

* Sales by Category
* Profit by Region
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales Trend by Year
* Profit vs Discount Analysis
* Sales Heatmap by Region and Category

## Key Insights

* Technology products generate the highest revenue.
* The Central region contributes the largest share of sales.
* Higher discounts are strongly associated with lower profitability.
* Sales increased consistently from 2011 to 2014.

## Conclusion

This analysis highlights important factors influencing retail performance and demonstrates how data analytics can support better business decisions.
